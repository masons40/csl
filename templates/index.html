Children’s School Lives (CSL)

Children's School Lives is a new longitudinal study commissioned by the National Council for Curriculum and Assessment (NCCA) and conducted by UCD School of Education. The study will explore the lived experiences of children in Irish primary schools from Spring 2019 to Spring 2024, providing Ireland’s first nationally representative study of primary schooling.
